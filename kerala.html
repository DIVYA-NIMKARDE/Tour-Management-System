KERALA

From the beautiful backwaters to the various hill stations to the awesome waterfalls, there’s so much to explore in Kerala. The vast network of brackish lagoons called backwaters, ayurvedic massages, wildlife sanctuaries, sprawling tea gardens, hill-stations, palm tree-lined beaches, and colorful dance forms, Kerala has everything on offer to take your pick from! Learn how to get out of your comfort zone, do things on your own, and sometimes you can even get to surprise yourself. The southernmost district and the capital of Kerala, God’s own country, one who visits Thiruvananthapuram, visits heaven experiencing the ecstasy of being here.“
